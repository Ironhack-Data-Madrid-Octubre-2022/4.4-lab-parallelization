      MediaWiki is a collaboration and documentation platform brought to you by a vibrant community.

      \n
      \n

      The MediaWiki software is used by tens of thousands of websites and thousands of companies and organizations.\nIt powers Wikipedia and also this website.\nMediaWiki helps you collect and organize knowledge and make it available to people.\nIt\'s powerful, multilingual, free and open, extensible, customizable, reliable, and free of charge.\nFind out more and if MediaWiki is right for you.\n
