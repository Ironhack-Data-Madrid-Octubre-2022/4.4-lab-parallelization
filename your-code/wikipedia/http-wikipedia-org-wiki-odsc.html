\n

Open Data Science Conference, or ODSC, is an annual event held in Boston,[1] San Francisco, Brazil, London, and India.[2] The purpose of ODSC events is to discuss data science and machine learning topics, as well as provide training sessions.\n

\n

History[edit]

\n

Open Data Science Conference was founded in 2015, with the first inaugural event held on May 30, 2015.[3] Since then, the event has grown rapidly, from 2,000 participants in 2017 to 8,230 registered attendees in 2020.[4][5]\n
